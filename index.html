What it IS and ISN'T...

  • It IS... A continuation of refinement to nail down the scope completely prior to coding or test planning
  • It ISN'T... A demo of the dev work that has already been completed
  • It IS... At the start of work on a story
  • It's ISN'T... After the dev work has already been completed
  • It IS... Needed again after diving deeper and discover additions
  • It IS... A meeting for the entire team
  • It IS... A discussion of work impact on other Boards (i.e. not just relying on the PO)
